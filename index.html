About ClicknDeliver


The app allows you to request a courier to collect and deliver a small to medium-sized parcel, from one pick up point to a single destination or from one pick-up point to multiple destinations.



How It Works

Download The ClicknDeliver App

Download the app from the iOS App Store or
the Google Playstore.

Register with your details

Register with your details and provide
the necessary information.

Request a driver for a parcel delivery

You can now request a courier to make a delivery by following the easy steps in the app. Enter your pick up location, enter your drop off location, choose a payment option and begin the trip.

Get delivery confirmation, track your courier, and get notified on delivery

Once you have requested your courier for delivery, you'll get confirmation, track your courier and recieve a notifictaion as soon as it your parcel is delivered.
